who is JAMES BOND

007

James Bond earns his 00 status and is sent to defeat terrorist banker Le Chiffre in a high-stakes poker game. Along the way, he falls for Vesper Lynd, whose betrayal and tragic death shape his future as 007

Still grieving Vesper, Bond uncovers a secret organization called Quantum while tracking down her killers. His mission leads to a vengeful showdown in Bolivia against Dominic Greene, who is manipulating a country's water supply

Bond's loyalty to M is tested when MI6 comes under attack from former agent Silva, who seeks revenge. The fight becomes deeply personal as Bond protects M, leading to a final showdown at his childhood home, Skyfall

Bond discovers that all his previous enemies were linked to the sinister organization SPECTRE, led by Ernst Stavro Blofeld. The revelations force Bond to confront his past while protecting Madeleine Swann, the woman he grows to love.

Bond comes out of retirement when a deadly bioweapon threatens millions, and he must face both SPECTRE and the new villain Safin. In a heartbreaking finale, Bond sacrifices himself to save Madeleine and their daughter, giving his life for love and duty.
